173 police officers and 78 fire fighters lost their lives in the line of duty in 2011. There are more than 581,000 police and 30,000 fire departments active in the United States every day serving millions of people. As citizens, they have gone above and beyond. These men, women, and departments deserve national recognition. By selling eye-catching and independently designed clothing, Siren Apparel will create this national recognition and donate to these heroes.
